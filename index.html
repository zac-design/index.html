How VoxAero Works

Real-time AI conversations: Not pre-recorded scripts. VoxAero's AI responds dynamically to YOUR radio calls, just like real ATC.
Full flight simulation: Practice from engine start to shutdown, or jump into any phase of flight.
Instant feedback: VoxAero identifies mistakes in phraseology, readbacks, and timing—then shows you how to fix them.
Structured debrief: After each session, review what went well and what needs work.
Practice with Tower, Radio, Information, and other ATC services: Train across multiple airport types and service levels.
Customizable scenarios: Choose your departure and destination, weather conditions, and traffic density.
Track your progress: See your improvement over time with detailed performance analytics.
Practice YOUR routes: Fly from your home strip to anywhere you'd normally go.
